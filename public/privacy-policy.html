Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2024
Last Updated: 01 January 2026

Lipi ("Lipi", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications, website, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Account credentials
  • Payment-related identifiers (processed via app stores; we do not store card details)

1.2 Usage Data

We collect information about how you use our Services, such as:

  • Games played and features used
  • Progress, scores, streaks, and preferences
  • Interaction logs and session duration

1.3 Device Information

We may collect device-related information including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system and app version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • IP address and network information

1.4 Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Improve app and website performance
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze usage trends

Cookies may be session-based or persistent and may include third-party cookies.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Provide gameplay, features, and content
  • Enable offline gameplay and data synchronization
  • Communicate service-related updates

2.2 Advertising & Monetization

Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device IP address
  • Device type
  • Approximate location
  • Advertising identifiers

This data is used to display relevant or non-personalized ads.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
Ad Preferences: https://adssettings.google.com/

2.3 Analytics & Research

We use analytics tools (such as Google Analytics or Firebase Analytics) to:

  • Understand user behavior
  • Improve features and performance

Collected data may include usage patterns and device information.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR-Ready)

We process personal data based on:

  • User consent
  • Contractual necessity (to provide Services)
  • Legal obligations
  • Legitimate business interests, such as improving user experience

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share data only with:

  • Service providers (analytics, hosting, notifications)
  • Advertising partners (Google AdMob)
  • Legal authorities, if required by law

All third parties are required to process data securely and only for specified purposes.

5. Data Storage & International Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in India or other countries where our service providers operate (including cloud infrastructure providers).

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border data transfers.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide Services
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes

Data is deleted or anonymized when no longer required.

7. User Rights

You have the right to:

7.1 Access & Correction

View and update your personal information through account settings.

7.2 Data Deletion

Request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7.3 Data Portability

Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.

7.4 Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable.

Requests will be processed in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Cookies Management

You can manage or disable cookies via:

  • Browser settings (for website)
  • Device settings (for mobile apps)

Disabling cookies may affect functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not intended for children under 13 years of age.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

Parents may contact us to:

  • Review their child's data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent

10. Security Measures

We use reasonable and industry-standard safeguards, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Restricted access controls
  • Regular security reviews

However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their policies separately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • App notifications

  • Website notice

Continued use of the Services after updates constitutes acceptance.
